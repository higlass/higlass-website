Summary

HiGlass is a tool for exploring large genomic data sets. It was created at the Gehlenborg Lab at Harvard Medical School as part of the 4D Nucleome Project's Data Coordination and Integration Center.

Citation

A preprint of the paper describing HiGlass is available on bioRxiv (https://doi.org/10.1101/121889). If you use HiGlass in your work, please cite:

Kerpedjiev, P., Abdennur, N., Lekschas, F., McCallum, C., Dinkla, K., Strobelt, H., ... & Gehlenborg, N. (2017). HiGlass: Web-based visual comparison and exploration of genome interaction maps. bioRxiv, 121889.
